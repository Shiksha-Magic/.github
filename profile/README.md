## 🏢 About Us

**Shiksha Magic** is an EdTech company building an all-in-one platform for coaching institutes, schools, tuition centers, and educational franchises across India and beyond.

We provide a complete digital infrastructure so that educators can focus on what matters most — **teaching** — while we handle everything else: student management, online exams, fee collection, attendance tracking, live classes, analytics, and even a fully branded mobile app for every institute.

---

## 🎯 What We Solve

Running an educational institute involves juggling dozens of disconnected tools — spreadsheets for attendance, WhatsApp for communication, manual receipts for fees, and no visibility into student performance. **Shiksha Magic replaces all of that with one unified platform.**

| Challenge | Our Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| Scattered student data | 📋 Centralized student, batch & course management |
| Manual attendance tracking | ✅ Digital attendance with parent notifications |
| Paper-based exams | 📝 Online test engine with auto-grading |

| No performance insights | 📊 Real-time analytics dashboards for all stakeholders |
| Generic branding | 📱 White-labeled mobile app with your institute's own brand |

## ✨ Platform Highlights

### 🏢 Institute Management
Complete branch management, role-based access for Admins, Teachers, Students, and Parents, with multi-tenant data isolation so every institute's data stays private and secure.

### 📱 Your Own Branded App
Every institute gets a **custom-branded mobile application** — with their logo, colors, and app name — published to Google Play and the App Store. No developer needed.

### 📝 Smart Examination System
Create and conduct online exams with MCQ, descriptive, numerical, and coding questions. Auto-grading saves hours of manual correction. Students get instant results.

### 💰 Fee & Finance Management
Define fee categories, allocate fees per student, track payments, generate receipts, and get a clear view of outstanding dues — all in one place.

### 📊 Actionable Analytics
Performance dashboards for every role — Administrators see institute-wide trends, Teachers track batch progress, Students monitor their growth, and Parents stay informed.

### 📹 Live Classes
Integrated with Zoom and Jitsi for seamless virtual classrooms. Schedule, notify, and conduct classes without leaving the platform.
